About Me

Born in Turin (Italy), though I spent most of my childhood studying in International Schools in Nanjing and Shanghai (China).

Graduated from Polytechnic University of Turin in 2020 with a diploma in Design and Visual Communication, specializing in Graphic and Virtual Design.

I've had a strong passion in Game Design, specifically Video Game Design, for many years. This passion made me study the subject on my own, pushing me to start a few projects that taught me how to give life to my ideas. The project I'm most proud of is Repair Rampage, a game made in 48 hours during the 2020 Global Game Jam.
